DAY 1

Day 1 of the soft skills training program focused on time management, crisis management, planning, and adaptability. Participants prioritized tasks and learned efficient time utilization. Crisis management strategies emphasized handling challenges calmly. Planning skills included setting clear goals and creating structured plans. Adaptability training highlighted adjusting to changes and maintaining flexibility. These skills improved personal growth, productivity, and teamwork. Through interactive activities and discussions, participants gained practical insights and tools. By the session's end, they were better equipped to manage time, handle crises, strategize, and adapt effectively.
